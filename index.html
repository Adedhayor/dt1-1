Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about importing cars from China

The typical timeline is 45–75 days from winning an auction to receiving your car in Lagos. This includes: auction processing (3–5 days), pre-shipping inspection (3–7 days), sea freight from China (25–40 days), and Nigerian port clearance (7–14 days). We keep you updated at every stage.

CIF Lagos (Cost, Insurance, Freight) includes the auction purchase price, export fees in China, international sea freight, and marine insurance. Nigerian customs duties, port handling charges, clearing agent fees, and last-mile delivery are charged separately and clearly quoted upfront.

Yes. All vehicles come with a detailed auction condition report including photos and a grade score (1–5). For premium purchases, we arrange a third-party mechanical inspection at the Chinese auction yard before shipping. Reports are shared via WhatsApp or email.

We import a wide range: clean-title used vehicles, salvage/rebuild vehicles, brand-new ex-China models, SUVs, sedans, pickup trucks, vans, buses, and electric vehicles. Certain prohibited vehicle categories under Nigerian regulations cannot be imported — we advise on eligibility during consultation.

Payments are made in instalments: a deposit to secure your bid, then full payment before shipping. We accept Nigerian bank transfers. All payments are documented with receipts and a formal import contract. We've facilitated 2,000+ successful imports — your investment is protected.

Most vehicles from Chinese auctions are left-hand drive (LHD), which is standard in Nigeria. We can also source right-hand drive vehicles from Japanese auctions on request, though availability varies. During your consultation, we'll clarify what's available for your preferred model.

Every vehicle is insured during transit. If any damage occurs during shipping, our marine insurance covers repairs or replacement value. We also conduct a thorough inspection at the Lagos port before handover and share photos with you. Your investment is protected end-to-end.
